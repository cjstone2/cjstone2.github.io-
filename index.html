Welcome to my website. here you can learn about my fitness journey and some tips I have that will hopefully help you.

Carson Stone is an undergraduate student at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Physical fitness has played a pivotal role in his college experience. Through this website, he aims to share the insights gained from his fitness journey, including valuable tips he learned and mistakes he made. His goal is to inspire and guide others in their pursuit of a balanced and healthy lifestyle during their academic careers.
