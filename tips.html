Tips

Getting Started: Choosing an activity, be it running, lifting, or any other form of exercise, often presents the initial challenge of determining where to start. My advice is to seek out individuals with expertise. Whether through a club, a group, or a friend, finding experienced practitioners can really help you learn your chosen activity safely and quickly. This guidance is particularly crucial in sports where proper form is essential, such as weightlifting. Learning from people who have good techniques can not only improve your performance but also lower the risk of injury.
